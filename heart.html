POUR THE PERFECT HEART 🧡


Step 1


Begin by steaming your milk until it's silky smooth.


When done give your pitcher a few taps on the counter to even out the texture and remove any bubbles.

Step 2


Pour the milk into the coffee, making sure that the pitcher is roughly 4 inches above the surface of the coffee.

Step 3


When you have filled the coffee mug to roughly 50% bring your pitcher as close as you can to the surface of the coffee without it touching.


This will allow the milk to sit on the top of the coffee.

Step 4


When you've just about filled the coffee mug, bring the pitcher back and up to create the heart shape in the milk.
