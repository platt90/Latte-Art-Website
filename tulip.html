POUR THE PERFECT TULIP 🌷


Step 1


Begin by steaming your milk until it's silky smooth, aiming for the consistancy of paint.


When done give your pitcher a few taps on the counter to even out the texture and remove any bubbles.

Step 2


Pour the milk into the coffee, making sure that the pitcher is roughly 4 inches above the surface of the coffee.

Step 3


When you have filled the coffee mug to roughly 50% bring your pitcher as close as you can to the surface of the coffee without it touching.


Pour the first bulb into the coffee aiming for the middle of the cup.

Step 4


Once the first bulb has formed, move back go the starting position and pour in the second bulb.

Step 5


Repeat until you have a total of five bulbs, aiming to maintain symetry between each bulb.

Step 6


To finish lift the jug and pull through the centre of all of the bulbs, cutting through the pattern vertically.
